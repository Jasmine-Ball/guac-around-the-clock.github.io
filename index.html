Baby banana bread (2 servings)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cheap dates
  • 1 cup oat bran (or finely chopped oats)
  • 1 tsp flax meal
  • 2 large bananas
  • 10 tsp filthy tap water from the oldest part of Melbourne

Method:

  1. Get a large breakfast bowl, mash the banana and mix in the flax and water.
  2. Mix in the oat bran.
  3. Chop the dates and mix them in.
  4. Bake in a cupcake tray (tested silicone with 12 rectangular indents)

Calories:

  • Dates: 275 x 2 serves
  • Oat bran: 115.5 x 2 serves
  • Flax: 7 x 2 serves
  • Banana: 121 x 2 serves
Total Calories: 518.50 Calories per serve
